About Us

We at Herbal Tea, are a team of pioneers in the fields of Ayurveda, and the food and beverage industry. As Ayurvedic practitioners for over eleven generations, we have studied, practiced and practically tested the boundaries of this knowledge for years. Add to that our strong lineage in the food and beverage industry, and you can be assured that our products come with a seal of authenticity and expertise.

We hope that through Herbëa, people are able to experience first-hand, the depth of Ayurveda, and the place it can have in our modern lifestyles.

Ayurveda needs to be shackled free from the confined spaces of trending health content and Herbëa is our way to help spread the roots of this ancient knowledge, allowing people to better understand it.
